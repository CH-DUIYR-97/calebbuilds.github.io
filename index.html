About Me

I'm a regular guy in my late 20s who recently got pulled into the world of coding. What started as a spark of curiosity turned into something deeper — a quiet, curiosity-driven obsession.


I'm inspired by the greats: Tope Awotona (Calendly), Jan Koum (WhatsApp), and Vinay Hiremath (Loom) — builders who created tools that made a real difference in people's lives.


The idea that lines of code, written with clarity and purpose, can impact millions is something I haven't been able to let go of.


I'm so early on this journey. I don't come from a technical background. I'm learning one line at a time. I believe in using technology to solve problems that matter — to make the world a little better, and the human race a little kinder, one line of code at a time.


At the end of the day, I'm just a big kid chasing a quiet dream — a coding noob trying to get good.
